Honey Walnut Shrimp

Ingredients Required:

- 1 cup water - 2/3 cup white sugar - 1/2 cup walnuts - 4 egg whites - 2/3 cup glutinous rice flour - 1/4 cup mayonnaise - 1 pound large shrimps, peeled and deveined - 2 tablespoons honey - 1 tablespoon milk - 1 cup vegetable oil

Special Tools Required:

- small saucepan - aluminum foil sheet - skillet - slotted spoon (tools can be substituted with a tool that functions similarly)

How To Cook It:

Step 1: Stir together the water and sugar in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil and add the walnuts. Boil for 2 minutes, then drain and place walnuts on aluminum foil sheet to dry. Step 2: Whip egg whites in a medium bowl until foamy. Stir in the mochiko until it has a pasty consistency. Heat the oil in a heavy deep skillet over medium-high heat. Dip shrimp into the mochiko batter, and then fry in the hot oil until golden brown, about 5 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Step 3: In a medium serving bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, honey and milk. Add shrimp and toss to coat with the sauce. Sprinkle the walnuts on top and serve.
