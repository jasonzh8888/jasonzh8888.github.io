Orange Chicken

Ingredients Required:

- 1 cup orange juice - 1/3 cup brown sugar - 1/3 cup rice vinegar - 2 tablespoons fish sauce - 1 tablespoon soy sauce - 1 tablespoon fresh ginger root, grated - 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes, crushed - 2 pounds chicken thighs, skinless, boneless, cut into chunks - 1 bunch green onions, sliced, white area and top separated - 1 fresh jalapeno pepper, sliced into rings - 1 red bell pepper, cut into 2 inch pieces - 1/2 cup sugar snap peas / snow peas - 4 cloves garlic, minced - 2 tablespoons orange zest, grated - 1 bunch cilantro leaves

Special Tools Required:

- colander - skillet (tools can be substituted with a tool that functions similarly)

How To Cook It:

Step 1: Whisk orange juice, brown sugar, rice vinegar, fish sauce, soy sauce, ginger, and crushed red pepper flakes in a large bowl. Step 2: Mix in chicken pieces and toss to evenly coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator for 1 hour. Step 3: Remove chicken from refrigerator and drain thoroughly in colander, reserving marinade. Step 4: Heat a large non-stick skillet over high heat. Cook and stir chicken for 2 minutes; spoon out any excess liquid. Step 5: Continue to cook and stir until chicken caramelizes, 6 to 7 minutes. Step 6: Stir in white portions of green onions, garlic, and orange zest; cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes. Step 7: Pour in half of reserved marinade. Simmer until reduced and thickened, 3 to 4 minutes. Step 8: Stir in jalapeno pepper, bell pepper, and sugar snap peas; cook and stir until vegetables are warmed, about 2 minutes. Step 9: Stir in green portions (tops) of green onions; cook and stir 1 minute. Step 10: Remove from heat. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
